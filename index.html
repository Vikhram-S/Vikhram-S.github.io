Vikhram S

AI Researcher | Multimodal Intelligence, Medical AI, and Public-Interest Technology

Vikhram S is an AI researcher working on multimodal intelligence, vision–language models, and explainable machine learning, with applications in medicine, law, and public safety. He earned a B.E. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Saveetha Engineering College, India, in 2026.

His research on explainable vision–language models for dermatological diagnosis is published in Springer's Lecture Notes in Networks and Systems. He is the lead author of a chapter selected for the India AI Impact Summit 2026 Compendium on AI and Gender Empowerment, a joint initiative of India's Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology and UN Women, chosen as one of 23 case studies from over 235 global submissions. He also created IndianConstitution, an open source legal NLP package exceeding 31,000 downloads, and advises Tech Society at Saveetha Engineering College on machine learning research.
